Gotland runt 2023

This year we had as much as over 300 participants in gotland runt offshore race. This years race had strong winds and really put all the boats to test, over 150 boats quit the race before crossing the baltic sea

Classics boats in the race.

New for this year is that all the classic boats gets their own track, they dont have to round the island instead they go out to alma grundet and set their course to Visby where they turn around and head back to sandhamn.

Strong winds and damaged boats

The gale force winds really put the boats to test. And a lot of boats got damaged, 2 boats broke their masts and 1 boat lost their rudder an had to be abandoned at sea. Boj boj who where in the lead broke their mast just outside fårö and therefore had to quit the race.

Crew got recued at sea

Team Pandion lost their rudder and in the string winds the situation got really dangerous so swedish coastguard rescued the crew who unfortetly had to abandoned their boat. When the wind and sea conditions where better a search team found Pandion drifting just outside estonia and she where towed back to stockholm.

Allegra wins her class

The biggest catamaran in the race was the first boat to finish the race and she did that in style, 12 hours before the rest of the fleet, she won her class.

Wet Job was really flying

Team wet job has been sailing together for years and really know their boat well. Wet job had a blast in these strong winds and when they rounded the southern part of gotland the boat really had all the winds she was built for and where flying.

The winner of the race

This years overall winner is wetjob who even thou sha had some mishaps with an undfortiently jibe on her way home wich cost her alot of valuable time managed to keep her lead all the way till the Finnish line. Good job Wetjob!

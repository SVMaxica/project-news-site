Gotland runt 2023

This year we had as much as over 300 participants in gotland runt offshore race. This years race had strong winds and really put all the boats to test, over 150 boats quit the race before crossing the baltic sea

Classics boats in the race.

New for this year ist that all the classic boats gets their own track, they dont have to round the island instead they go out to alma grundet and set their course to Visnby where they turn around and head back to sandhamn.
